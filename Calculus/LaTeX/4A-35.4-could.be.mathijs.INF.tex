$\intt7^{2x+5}$\\

Stel $t = 2x+5 \Rightarrow dt = 2dx \Rightarrow dx = \dfrac{1}{2} dt$\\

$\intt7^{2x+5} = \intt 7^t \dfrac{1}{2} dt = \dfrac{1}{2} \intt  7^t dt = \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \dfrac{7^t}{\ln 7} + c = \dfrac{7^{2x+5}}{2\ln 7} + c$ met $c \in \mathbb{R}$
